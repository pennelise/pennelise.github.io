I am currently a research scientist at Zeus AI developing our observation-to-observation AI air quality model.

I obtained my Ph.D. from Harvard University, where I worked with Daniel Jacob and Carbon Mapper, Inc. to on methane emissions using Bayesian inverse methods, and spent 6 months as a data science intern at Tesla for the Supercharger team. Before graduate school, I was fortunate to work with Tracey Holloway at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, at the NOAA Geophyisical Fluid Dynamics Laboratory with Larry Horowitz and Vaishali Naik, and at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory with Dr. Jennifer Newman.
